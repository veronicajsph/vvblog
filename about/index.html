About Veronica's Voice

Hi, and welcome to my blog, Veronica's Voice! As you can guess, my name is Veronica, and I consider myself to be a passionate writer and a curious thinker. I created this blog so I could share my thoughts on literally anything that I've gotten interested in, from personal experiences to social issues, and everything in between.

When I'm not building this website or writing articles, you can probably find me with my head buried in a book (my current favorite is Crying in H-Mart), walking my dog Bentley, taking care of my family, or traveling around NYC trying to find my new favorite restaurant!

I started building this website because I wanted to create my own online haven where I could, in all honesty, ramble about anything I wanted to. Whether if it's through articles that invoke serious conversations or a random yet exciting topic , I hope that any readers can enjoy learning about these subjects as much as I enjoyed writing about them.
